About The Role

We're looking for a Senior Product Manager to lead our user enablement and growth domain ensuring employees have the right access and context to spend in line with their business interests, maximising their access to all that Pleo has to offer . This pivotal role will define and execute the product vision and strategy to transform inefficient spend controls into an effortless and empowered experience. With the right access to spend for their needs and rich guidance and context for how to spend we aim to remove the headache of controlling spend, all without losing control.

Your empathy for Finance and HR professionals is crucial, as this team operates at the interface of company structure and financial access, this is balanced with your commercial acumen where you've directly influenced and led growth strategies . We are looking for someone who is exceptional at getting to the root cause of problems and can understand the motivation of finance professionals in order to challenge the status quo, with humility and clarity.

What you'll be doing

As a Senior Product Manager for the User & Controls team, you will:

Lead the product vision, strategy, and roadmap for our User Management and Spend Controls space, ensuring we sustain our market-leading position and experience 
Bring domain expertise in user permissions, access controls, spend policies, budget enforcement, and approval workflows, to directly influence the product roadmap 
Play a pivotal role in increasing our ARPA by giving mid-market finance admins the confidence that all employees who need to spend should do so through Pleo 
Engage stakeholders from our Product Marketing & GTM domains to drive adoption and growth campaigns, ensuring that all employees have a good knowledge of Pleo's different spend products 
Collaborate with your engineering, design, and data counterparts to align on strategies and ensure seamless execution of product initiatives 
Leverage data and qualitative user research to deeply understand user needs and pain points related to managing users and controlling spend, translating these insights into innovative product solutions 
Identify market trends, competitive landscapes, and potential partnerships to inform long-term product strategy and future investment into the space 

What you bring

You'll thrive in this role if you have:

Experience from HRIS and user management, either building solutions or previous experience working towards an HR team and ideally also a Finance team. 
Visionary and innovative with a proven track record of finding novel solutions to known problems to make sure we stay category defining. 
A results-oriented growth mindset, comfortable with ambiguity and a relentless drive for measurable outcomes that see the need to collaborate across the org as an opportunity, not an obstacle. 
Strong track record of leading high-performing cross-functional product teams (>10 engineers) through exceptional product management craft and leadership 
Exceptional at breaking down complex concepts, prioritising work and conveying a clear narrative to your team and stakeholders. 
Deep customer empathy and proven experience conducting user research to translate insights into tangible product improvements 
Experience in working with AI and automation to improve workflows is beneficial 

Who you'll be working with and reporting to

You'll report to our Senior Product Director Rob Guard and work closely with your Engineering Manager, two designers and tech leads. Together with your EM and Design lead, you set the strategy, direction and priority for two engineering squads. You'll also have the chance to partner with members from Data, Product Research, GTM and Partnerships to drive the success of your product.
