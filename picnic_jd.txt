About the job
In a nutshell

Are you ready to redefine daily life for millions? At Picnic, we're not just delivering groceries; we're building the future of commerce through groundbreaking tech and sustainable innovation. If you're a product visionary passionate about shaping hyper-personalized experiences that truly matter, then let's talk.

Tricks of the trade

Uncover Truths: Dive deep into customer behavior and data to identify pivotal opportunities and forge a product roadmap that balances user needs with business goals.
Design Futures: Partner with UX designers to map customer journeys and sculpt features that elevate every touchpoint: from intuitive shopping and flawless delivery to effortless aftercare.
Empower & Scale: Cultivate a high-performing product team, fostering collaboration and excellence within a rapidly expanding, agile ecosystem.

Your superpowers

Academic Firepower: Master's degree in a quantitative or technical field (e.g., Mathematics, Computer Science, Data Science, Engineering).
Proven Impact: 2-5 years of experience in a fast-paced, product-led organization, ideally with a track record as a Product Analyst, Product Owner, or Product Manager.
Analytical Maverick: Possess sharp analytical and conceptual thinking, always rooting decisions in customer understanding, rigorous data, and technical feasibility.
Experience Obsessed: Deeply committed to shipping products that aren't just functional, but genuinely delightful and impactful.
Collaboration Catalyst: An exceptional communicator and collaborator, adept at uniting diverse stakeholders around a shared vision.
Ready to Roll: Hold an EU working permit or citizenship.
